## Scenario Questions

### Scenario 1

- Scanner reports a high-severity SQL injection finding with medium confidence.

- Describe how you would validate the issue before including it in a report.

---

### Scenario 2

- A scan completes without identifying any vulnerabilities.

- Would you conclude the application is secure? Why or why not?

---

### Scenario 3

## Interview Tips

- Strong interview answers usually:

  * Explain the objective before the tool.
  * Distinguish findings from confirmed vulnerabilities.
  * Emphasize manual validation.
  * Mention evidence rather than severity alone.
  * Show awareness of scope and authorization.

- Interviewers often value sound reasoning more than tool-specific terminology.

---

## Rapid Revision

- Remember these five principles:

  1. Define scope carefully.
  2. Configure intentionally.
  3. Review evidence.
  4. Validate manually.
  5. Report confirmed findings only.
