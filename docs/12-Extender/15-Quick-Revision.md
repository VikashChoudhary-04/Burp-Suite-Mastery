## What Is Burp Extender?

- Burp Extender allows Burp Suite to gain additional capabilities through extensions.

- These extensions help solve specific problems such as:

  * Hidden parameter discovery
  * JWT analysis
  * Authorization testing
  * Request logging
  * Workflow automation
  * Specialized security analysis

- Remember:

  > **Extensions support your methodology—they do not replace it.**

## Core Concepts

- Remember these terms:

  * **Extension** — Adds new functionality to Burp Suite.
  * **BApp Store** — Curated source of Burp extensions.
  * **Compatibility** — Ensures the extension works with your Burp environment.
  * **Dependencies** — Supporting components an extension may require.
  * **Configuration** — Settings that determine how an extension behaves.
  * **Maintenance** — Ongoing review, updates, and removal of extensions.

## Professional Principles

- Experienced testers:

  * Install extensions intentionally.
  * Understand manual workflows before automating.
  * Validate extension output.
  * Keep toolkits focused.
  * Review extensions regularly.

- Good tooling supports good methodology.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Avoid:

  * Installing every available extension.
  * Blindly trusting automation.
  * Ignoring compatibility.
  * Skipping documentation.
  * Leaving unused extensions installed.
  * Troubleshooting without a systematic process.

---

## Decision Checklist

- Before installing any extension, ask:

  * What problem am I solving?
  * Can Burp already do this?  
  * Does the extension provide real value?
  * Is it compatible with my environment?
  * How will I validate its results?
  * Will I continue using it after this engagement?

- If you cannot answer these questions confidently, reconsider the installation.
