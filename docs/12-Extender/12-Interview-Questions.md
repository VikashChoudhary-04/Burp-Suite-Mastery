## Scenario Questions

### Scenario 1

- You need to discover hidden HTTP parameters across a large application.

- How would you decide whether an extension is appropriate?

---

### Scenario 2

- A colleague recommends installing ten popular Burp extensions before beginning a penetration test.

- Would you do this? Why or why not?

---

### Scenario 3

- An extension reports a possible security issue.

- Describe how you would validate the finding before including it in a report.

---

### Scenario 4

- Burp becomes noticeably slower after several new extensions are installed.

- Describe your troubleshooting process.

---

### Scenario 5

- A previously useful extension is no longer maintained.

- How would you decide whether to continue using it, replace it, or remove it?

## Interview Tips

- Strong answers usually:

  * Start with the security objective.
  * Explain the reasoning behind the decision.
  * Mention validation.
  * Consider performance and maintenance.
  * Demonstrate practical experience rather than memorized definitions.

- Interviewers often care more about your thought process than your ability to list extension names.

---

## Rapid Revision

- Remember these five principles:

  1. Solve the problem first.
  2. Install intentionally.
  3. Validate automation.
  4. Keep the toolkit lean.
  5. Review and maintain regularly.
